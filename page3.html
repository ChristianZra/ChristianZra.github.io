Information about class:

Anthropology:
We learn about the human
culture, evolution and diversity.
We learn how different societies
function and how the people in
the different places work. This
can help you with your social
skills. You can talk to more
people from different places
and really understand them.

Archeology:
We learn about artifacts
and fossils. In this class,
we learn about the history
of certain valuable objetcs
and we learn how to analyze
and interpret the valuable
very old artifacts to find out
how old they are and how
much they are worth.

Economics:
We learn about the
production, distribution
and the consumption of
money, goods and services.
In this class we learn and
study the differnet types
of economic systems and
what role they play on society.

Geography:
We learn about the physical
and cultural features of the
land of our earth. In this class
we learn about landforms,
population and climates of
different areas. In this class
it is also important to study
maps and how to use them.

History
We learn about the
significant events and
people from the past.
We learn about political,
social and cultural events
and the history of them.
We also learn about the
different eras in the world
and how they shaped the
world we live in now.

Law:
We learn about different
types of laws. How the
court works and the
importance of following
the law, and helping others
follow the law. If you see
someone breaking the law,
with this class you will know,
and you can help bring the
criminals to justice.

Linguistic:
We learn about the
strurcture of language
and how to analyze and
interpret text, including
old text. In this class we
also learn about different
types of language's
grammar rules and how to
apply them to the writing.
We also cover the literary
devices and how to
identify them.

Politics:
We learn about the
government and how
it works. In this class
we cover the different
government branches
and all of their
responsibilities. In this
class we also learn about
the different view points of
each branch of government.

Psychology:
We learn about the human
mind, how it works and its
behaviour. In this class we
cover different psychological
theories and how to apply
them to real life situations.
We also learn about different
types of mental illnesses and
how they affect the human brain.

Sociology:
We learn about society and
human behaviour. In this class
we cover different social
structures and how they influence
mental behaviour. We also
learn about the different social
issues, such as poverty, inequality
and discrimination.
