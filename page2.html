Combat Club

Arkham Academy's very own Combat Club

Information

The Combat Club in Arkham Academy is a very diverse club. In this club, the leaders teach the students combat. This club is not mandatory, and is purely 100% the students choice. In this club, the students spar against each other, and their senseis for practice, of course after they learn. The students also go to tournaments, exhibitions, and regular matches, either at home, or at other schools.
